FAQ

Common Questions.

What formats are included?

Logo templates come as AI (Adobe Illustrator), EPS, SVG, and high-resolution PNG. Social media packs include editable Figma source files and Canva template links. Typography products are PDF guides.

Can I use these for client work?

Yes. All paid products include a commercial licence — you can use, adapt, and deliver them to clients. The only restriction is reselling or redistributing the source files themselves.

What software do I need?

Logo and brand kit files require Adobe Illustrator CS6+ or a compatible vector application (Affinity Designer, CorelDRAW). Social packs require Figma (free) or Canva. Typography guides are standard PDF.

Do you offer refunds?

Because these are instant-download digital products, we cannot offer refunds once a file has been downloaded. If you have a technical issue or received a corrupted file, email hello@craftsynk.com and we'll sort it immediately.

How is payment handled?

All purchases are processed securely by Gumroad — a trusted platform for digital creators. Craftsynk never sees or stores your card details. Gumroad accepts all major cards and PayPal.
