Overview

Craftsynk ("we", "us", "our") operates this website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when you visit or interact with this site. We take your privacy seriously — we collect only what we need and never sell your data.

Short version: We collect contact form submissions and basic analytics. We don't sell your data, we don't spam, and you can ask us to delete your information at any time.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you submit the contact form or subscribe through Logo Lab, we collect:

  • Name and email address
  • Company or brand name (optional)
  • Project details you share in your brief
  • Budget and timeline preferences
  • How you heard about us

Information collected automatically

When you visit this website, standard server and analytics tools may collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website
  • General geographic region (country/city level)

We do not collect precise location data, device identifiers, or any information beyond what is necessary for operating and improving this site.

How We Use It

Information collected is used solely to:

  • Respond to project inquiries submitted through the contact form
  • Deliver Logo Lab downloads to subscribers
  • Send occasional design-related updates (subscribers only — you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Understand how visitors use this site to improve it

We do not use your information for automated decision-making, profiling, or targeted advertising. Every email from Craftsynk is personally reviewed and sent by a human.

Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Third-party tools that help us operate (e.g., Formspree for form processing). These providers are contractually bound to handle data securely and only as instructed.
  • Legal compliance: If required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Business transfer: In the unlikely event of a merger or acquisition, user data would transfer subject to the same privacy commitments.

Cookies & Analytics

This site may use minimal cookies for functionality (e.g., remembering your theme preference). We use privacy-respecting analytics — if any — to understand aggregate visitor behaviour. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party trackers beyond what is operationally necessary.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Core site functionality will not be affected.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 24 months to allow us to follow up on inquiries and maintain project records. Subscriber emails are retained until you unsubscribe. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us at hello@craftsynk.com.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access — request a copy of the data we hold about you
  • Correction — request we fix inaccurate data
  • Deletion — request we erase your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured format
  • Objection — object to certain processing activities
  • Unsubscribe — opt out of marketing emails at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@craftsynk.com. We respond within 30 days.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. This site is served over HTTPS. Form submissions are processed by Formspree, which uses industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take reasonable precautions and will notify you of any breach that affects your data as required by applicable law.

Children

This website is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes are effective immediately upon posting to this page. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any revision. We encourage you to review this policy occasionally. Continued use of this site after changes constitutes acceptance.
