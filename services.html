Services

The full offering.

A brand identity system is not a logo. It is a structured set of visual decisions that define how your brand communicates across every platform, medium, and touchpoint — consistently and confidently.

This is the flagship service. It includes everything: strategy, mark design, typography, color, usage rules, and a complete asset library. When it's done, you have a system — not just files.

What's included
  • Brand strategy and positioning brief
  • Primary logo design (3+ concepts)
  • All logo variations and lockups
  • Color palette (primary, secondary, neutrals)
  • Typography system with hierarchy rules
  • Brand guidelines document (PDF)
  • Pattern or texture system (if applicable)
  • Application mockups (stationery, signage, digital)
  • All source files and export formats

The logo is not the brand — but it is the most important single element of the visual identity. It must work at 16px and 16 feet. It must function in black and white and in color. It must communicate the right things.

This service focuses on precision mark and wordmark creation, grounded in a strategic brief and refined through structured iteration.

What's included
  • Strategy brief and competitive research
  • 2–3 initial logo concepts
  • Refinement rounds
  • Primary, horizontal, and stacked lockups
  • Light, dark, and monochrome versions
  • Icon-only version
  • AI, SVG, PNG, PDF file delivery
  • Basic usage notes document

A brand system is only as strong as the documentation that governs it. Brand guidelines define how every element is to be used — and how it must never be used. They protect brand equity as the company scales.

This service creates a professional brand standards document — suitable for handing to a marketing team, creative agency, developer, or printer.

What's included
  • Logo usage rules and clear space
  • Color specifications (RGB, CMYK, HEX, Pantone)
  • Typography system and hierarchy rules
  • Do's and don'ts (prohibited uses)
  • Application examples (digital and print)
  • Photography and imagery direction
  • Voice and tone guidelines (optional)
  • Delivered as a polished PDF document

A brand that exists only as a logo file is incomplete. Every touchpoint — the business card, the email signature, the presentation template, the social media profile — communicates the brand. It should do so with the same precision as the logo itself.

Asset types available
  • Business cards and stationery suite
  • Letterhead and envelope templates
  • Presentation / pitch deck template
  • Email signature design
  • Social media profile and cover templates
  • Branded document templates
  • Packaging design (print-ready)
  • Signage and environmental graphics
